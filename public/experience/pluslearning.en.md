# Co-founder · UX/UI Designer & Developer

**PlusLearning** · May 2025 — Mar 2026

PlusLearning is an English-learning **web & mobile app** startup that I co-founded with three teammates. We registered it as a household business (hộ kinh doanh) with **4 members** and took it from an idea to a working product.

## My role

- **Main UX/UI designer** — designed the full product experience in Figma, from user flows to high-fidelity screens and a reusable component system.
- Built the **mobile app** with **React Native**.
- Built the **website** with **React**.
- Worked end to end across design and development, turning my own designs into shipped features.

## What I did

- Shaped the product direction and the learning experience together with the founding team.
- Designed and implemented the interface, keeping it consistent across mobile and web.
- Developed app and web features, integrated them, and iterated based on feedback.

## What I learned

- How to take a product from zero to launch as part of a small founding team.
- Owning both **design and implementation** and keeping them in sync.
- Making product and scope decisions under real startup constraints.
