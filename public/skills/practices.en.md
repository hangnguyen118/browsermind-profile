# AI & Modern Practices

I actively apply AI and modern development practices to work faster and with higher quality.

## Prompt Engineering — *Advanced*

- Designing clear, structured prompts to get the most out of language models.
- Few-shot, chain-of-thought, and output-constraint techniques.

## Spec-Driven Development — *Intermediate*

- Starting from a clear specification before writing code.
- Reduces misunderstandings and makes work easier to test and maintain.

## AI-Assisted Development — *Intermediate*

- Using AI tools to speed up writing, reviewing, and refactoring code.
- Knowing when to trust AI output and when to verify it.

## Local AI — *Intermediate*

- Running models directly in the browser/device with no backend.
- This website runs Model + RAG entirely on the client side.

---

> I treat AI as a teammate — it lets me focus on design thinking instead of repetitive tasks.
